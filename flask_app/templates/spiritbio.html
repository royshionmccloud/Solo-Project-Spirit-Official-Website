Spirit Biography

“Spirit” Dance ministry is comprised of my sister Lanee Collins, and myself, Royshion McCloud. We began our praise dancing career in 1998 with dance group Daughters of Zion under the direction of Linda and Renada Newton until we parted in 2002. Later that year, at the request of the present Minister of Music of The Historic St. James AME Church, Bobby Richardson, to minister at a church event, my sister and I formed together as a duo, “Spirit”. Since that time, we have both honed our skills, dancing in multiple dance groups. We have ministered on big stages and little stages alike. We have had the opportunity to be apart of back-up praise dancing ministries for recording artist Donnie McClurkin, Kurt Carr, and local Christian rap artist G.LO. (God’s Love Only). We have been ministering in dance for over 15+ years.
